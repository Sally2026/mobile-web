Final Site Planning

Questionnaire

  1. Who is the target audience?

    2. My target audience consists of business owners, entrepreneurs, and professionals who need a web designer or developer build or enhance their online presence.

  2. How can I tailor the web site to reach that audience?

    3. I will design a clean, professional, and visually appealing website with a user-friendly layout, that highlights my services, and portfolio.

  3. What are the goals for the site?
    • To showcase my skills and portfolio.
    • To attract potential clients.
    • To provide information about my services.
    • To establish an online presence.
  4. How will I gather the information?

    5. I will research competitor websites, industry trends, and business needs to refine my approach.

  5. What are my sources for multimedia content?
    • My own designs and projects (logos, websites, branding materials).
    • Stock images and icons (from royalty-free sources)
    • Custom graphics and branding elements created in design software like Adobe Illustrator or Canva.
  6. What is my budget?

    7. Since this is my own website, I will keep costs minimal by using free or affordable resources (e.g., GitHub for hosting)

  7. How long do I have to complete the project?

    8. I plan to complete the website within 4-5 weeks.

  8. Who is on my project team?

    9. It is an individual project

  9. How often should the site be updated?

    10. The site should be regularly updated to reflect new projects, services, and industry trends.

  10. Who is responsible for updating the site?

    11. As the owner and designer, I will be responsible for maintaining and updating the site.
